About me

Hi! I'm a programmer working in data analytics at IBM. Previously a mechanical engineer and national gymnast.

Some of my current interests include: tools for thought (especially related to memory), critical rationalism, and progress (and how to make it).

Born and raise in Toronto, Canada.

What I'm doing right now

I've been playing with and really enjoying a writing tool called Roam. Using Roam has stirred up a lot of ideas around tools for thought, writing practices, and thinking environments. That's led to building some experimental tools and practices within Roam which I may share on here in the future. One such tool is ankify-roam which allows you to define flashcards inline as you write and then easily sync them to the Anki.

I'm trying to build and write in public. Even though I've read countless people advocating to do so and I agreed with all their reasons, I've always had trouble getting over the emotional discomfortable with the idea. This website, ankify_roam, and my slightly-above-lurking level of engagement on twitter are my first steps in that direction.
